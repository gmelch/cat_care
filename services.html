We offer a variety of services so that we can help you exactly how YOU need!

Daycare

Our daycare service provides care for no more than 24 hours. This option is great for those needing overnight or full day care for your pet! This service does include food and water but not grooming. Pricing for this service is done by the hour.



Weekend Care

Our weekend service provides care for up to 72 hours. This option is great for leaving town for the weekend and needing a place to care for your furry friend! This service includes food and water, grooming, and we will even send you a picture of your pet while you are away. Pricing for this service is done in twelve hour segments.



Extended Care

Our extended service provides care for more than 72 hours. This option is great for those who will be away for more than a few days and need someone to care for their pet! We want you to be able to rest easy while you're away without having to worry about the well-being of your animal. This service includes food and water, grooming, and a picture of your pet while you are away. Pricing for this service is done by the day. NOTE: we require that you leave us the contact of someone who will be in the city of Houston while you aware that we can contact in case of an emergency.
